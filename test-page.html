Social Responsibility

Connexta Gives Back

Connexta employees are often looking to give back to their community, spurring the launch of two new programs in 2018 to assist employees in giving back and facilitating company-wide fundraising events.

Charitable Match

Connexta matches employee contributions to charitable organizations, up to $200 per year.

Dollars for Doers

Connexta matches volunteer hours with a charitable donation. Employees log volunteer hours and can earn up to $750 per year to benefit a charitable organization.
